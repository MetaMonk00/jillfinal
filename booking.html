Before You Book

Good to Know

$25 Deposit Required

All new clients and prior no-shows must pay a $25 deposit at booking. Applied to your total.

24-Hour Cancellation

Less than 24 hrs notice = 50% charge. No-shows = 100% charge. Non-negotiable policy.

Late Pickup Fee

30-minute grace period. After that: $15–$25/hr late pickup fee.

Have Your Dog's Info Ready

Name, breed, weight, age, any health or behavioral notes. The more we know the better!
